How to keep your wife and nanny sane

Step 1: Unwrapping the Ephemeral Tooth-Picking Delight

Carefully remove the dental soft pick from its plastic packaging, marveling at its pristine, unused state. Savor this moment, for it shall be the only time you ever use this pick.

Step 2: Dramatic Dental Dislodging with Maximum Auditory Flair

Gingerly insert the pick between your teeth, waggling it around dramatically to really get in there and dislodge any pesky food particles. Make exaggerated "oooh" and "ahhh" sounds to heighten the experience.

Step 3: Bidding Farewell to Your Plastic Tooth-Picking Partner

Once satisfied that you've picked your teeth to perfection, promptly throw the pick in the trash, taking care to watch it land sadly at the bottom of the bin. Step 4: Repeat the Cycle of Dental Despair - Buy More Picks!

There you have it - the complete lifecycle of the humble dental soft pick, as meaningless and disappointing as life itself. Enjoy!

Dental Soft Picks

Dental soft picks are SINGLE-USE plastic toothpicks designed for quickly dislodging food from your teeth. You get to feel the fleeting satisfaction of picking your teeth with a fresh pick, before tossing it in the trash, never to be used again.
